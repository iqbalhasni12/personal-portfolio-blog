Smart Agriculture using ESP-NOW

Posted recently

The architecture features an ESP32 S3 acting as the central hub, communicating seamlessly with multiple ESP32 sensor nodes. This allows for efficient monitoring of soil conditions and irrigation management across a distributed area without relying on standard, power-heavy Wi-Fi networks.
